5 Things That Ruin a Dating Profile

1. Being Vague Instead of Specific
Generic = Forgettable
❌ Ruins It
"I love traveling, good food, and spending time with friends."
✓ Fixes It
"I've been to 12 countries. My favorite: hiking in Peru. I'm obsessed with hole-in-the-wall Thai restaurants, and my friends tease me for organizing group trips constantly."
Why: Specific details make YOU memorable. When someone reads your profile, they should imagine an actual date with you.
2. Putting Down What You're NOT Instead of What You ARE
Negatives = Red Flag
❌ Ruins It
"No drama. Not looking for games. No toxic people. Must be genuine."
✓ Fixes It
"I value directness over subtlety. I actually enjoy deep conversations where we disagree. Honesty is hotter than politeness."
Why: Listing what you don't want sounds defensive and exhausted. The right person will recognize themselves in your values.
3. Trying to Be Funny When You're Not
Forced Humor = Insecurity
❌ Ruins It
"Here for a good time, not a long time LOL. Let's grab pizza."
✓ Fixes It
If you're naturally funny, show it through real stories. If you're not, don't force it. Authenticity is more attractive.
Why: Trying to be someone you're not repels your actual match. Let your real personality shine.
4. Leading With What You WANT Instead of What You BRING
Requirements List = Turn Off
❌ Ruins It
"Looking for someone ambitious, fit, who can keep up with me. Must have these qualities."
✓ Fixes It
"I'm ambitious, fit, and always up for the next challenge. Looking for someone with their own drive."
Why: People are attracted to people excited about their own lives, not people listing requirements like a menu.
5. Hiding Behind Filtered Photos
Filters = Broken Promises
❌ Ruins It
All filtered photos. Professional headshots. Photos without showing your actual face.
✓ Fixes It
At least one clear, natural photo where someone can see the real you. Bonus if one shows you doing something you love.
Why: A filtered photo promises someone will be disappointed. Real photos set expectations and attract genuine matches.
